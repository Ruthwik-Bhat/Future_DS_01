# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## About
This project was completed as part of a Data Science & Analytics internship task.
It focuses on analyzing business sales performance using retail transaction data.

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales data to identify revenue trends,
top-selling products, and regional performance to support data-driven
business decision-making.

## Dataset
Online Retail Dataset (UCI Machine Learning Repository)

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

## Project Workflow
- Loaded and explored the sales dataset
- Cleaned the data by removing missing and invalid values
- Created a revenue feature for sales analysis
- Analyzed total revenue, top products, and country-wise performance
- Visualized monthly revenue trends and product sales
- Summarized key business insights and recommendations

## Key Insights
- A small number of products contribute to a large share of total revenue
- Sales show clear seasonal trends over time
- The United Kingdom is the highest revenue-generating country
- High-performing products drive overall business growth

## Recommendations
- Focus on inventory planning for top-selling products
- Increase marketing efforts during high-sales months
- Strengthen strategies in high-performing regions
- Improve customer retention for repeat buyers

## Conclusion
This analysis provides actionable insights that can help improve business sales
performance and support strategic decision-making.
